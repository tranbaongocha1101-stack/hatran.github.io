<br>

<div markdown="1">
  
### Scope

End-to-end design and delivery of custom geotechnical drilling rig with improved stability, serviceability, and operational capability.

### Key Contributions

- Led mechanical design of folding mast with custom hinge solution, rotating stabilizer legs and control console.
- Reduced centre of gravity through machine layout optimisation.
- Redesigned foot clamp and breakout assembly integrating custom hydraulic cylinders.
- Redesigned rubber track mounting using standard PFC profiles to improve structural consistency and reduce weight and fabrication cost.
- Converted mast travel mechanism from timing belt to leaf chain system for improved durability.
- Designed drill rod storage and mud pump mounting systems.   
- Performed hydraulic component selection, pipework routing, and sizing calculations.  
- Simulated operation and validated performance under representative loading.
- Developed automated BOM extraction tool (Solid Edge → Google Sheets).
- Delivered DFM-optimised fabrication and manufacturing drawings with GD&T, ensuring dimension accuracy, tolerances and compliance with relevant standards (particularly for components manufactured by overseas suppliers).
- Managed supplier engagement, cost negotiation, and manufacturing delivery.
- Prepared commissioning documentation and operator manuals.

### Outcomes

- Successful design, manufacture, and delivery of custom drilling rig meeting all client requirements. 
- Improved machine packaging, reliability, and serviceability.  
- Reliable and stable engine and hydraulic system performance.
