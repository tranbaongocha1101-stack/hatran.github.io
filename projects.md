    <div class="project-col">
      <h4>Key Contributions</h4>
      <ul>
        <li>Led mechanical design of folding mast with custom hinge solution, rotating stabilizer legs and control console.</li>
        <li>Reduced centre of gravity through machine layout optimisation.</li>
        <li>Redesigned foot clamp and breakout assembly integrating custom hydraulic cylinders.</li>
        <li>Redesigned rubber track mounting using standard PFC profiles to reduce weight and fabrication cost.</li>
        <li>Converted mast travel mechanism from timing belt to leaf chain system for improved durability.</li>
        <li>Designed drill rod storage and mud pump mounting systems.</li>
        <li>Performed hydraulic component selection, pipework routing, and sizing calculations.</li>
        <li>Developed automated BOM extraction tool (Solid Edge &rarr; Google Sheets).</li>
        <li>Delivered DFM-optimised fabrication drawings with GD&amp;T.</li>
        <li>Managed supplier engagement, cost negotiation, and manufacturing delivery.</li>
        <li>Prepared commissioning documentation and operator manuals.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="project-col">
      <h4>Outcomes</h4>
      <ul>
        <li>Successful design, manufacture, and delivery meeting all client requirements.</li>
        <li>Improved machine packaging, reliability, and serviceability.</li>
        <li>Reliable and stable engine and hydraulic system performance.</li>
      </ul>
