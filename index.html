About

I am a Mechanical Engineer with combined industry and research experience delivering complex mechanical systems across heavy industrial machinery, renewable energy, aerospace platforms, and marine engineering. I hold a PhD and Master’s in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Auckland and a Bachelor’s in Naval Architecture — a rare breadth spanning concept to commissioning across multiple engineering domains.

In industry I have led end-to-end design of custom geotechnical drilling rigs — from folding mast design and hydraulic system integration to supplier coordination and offshore deployment in the Solomon Islands. In research, I designed experimental wind tunnel rigs for NZ Defence Force UAV testing and published work on tidal turbine hydrodynamics. I thrive where precision engineering meets real-world constraints.
